Product Details and Pricing

Custom Braille or Large Print Labels

Price: $30

  • Great for machines with interfaces that are already mostly accessible, such as those that have only buttons
  • Available in Braille, tactile markings, or large print
  • Tactile markings are good for small buttons with no room for larger labels

VIPAP, an Accessible Website and Mobile App

Price: $50

  • Allows you to modify machine settings using technology you're already comfortable with
  • Works with your machine's built-in wireless connectivity or a device we provide
  • Can be used with multiple machines or easily transferred to a new machine
  • Works with all existing screen reading and magnification software

VIPAP Connect, A Physical Device that Plugs into Machine, Giving it Wireless Connectivity

Price: $100

  • Works with the VIPAP app
  • Gives machines without wireless connectivity the ability to work with the app
  • Plug and play: Plug the device into your machine and forget about it
